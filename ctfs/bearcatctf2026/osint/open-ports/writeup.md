## Solution

Searching for the IMO of 9281267 and MMSI of 354202000 gave me general
results relating to the information of this ship, but, based on my
initial search results, it seemed that, if I wanted to get location 
information, I would have to pay for it. I eventually got grok to find
a website that was giving out this information for free though:
https://globalfishingwatch.org/map/vessel-search

I then used this to find the city that the ship in question would
be docking at a few days after the logs were written.
